    About AtomTimer

    AtomTimer is an app created in order to help you work / study more efficiently. It is inspired by various studying techniques and helps you implement them into your studying or working sessions. It mainly focuses on spaced repetition, high intensity sessions and working on specific tasks. This app aims to boost your efficiency and help you make the most of your working / studying time.

    About the techniques

    Pomodoro Technique

    The Pomodoro Technique is created by Francesco Cirillo for a more productive way to work and study. The technique uses a timer to break down work into intervals, traditionally 25 minutes in length, separated by short breaks.
    Source: Wikipedia

    Learn more about this technique in the official website.

    Spaced Repetition

    Spaced repetition is an evidence-based learning technique that is usually performed with flashcards. Newly introduced and more difficult flashcards are shown more frequently, while older and less difficult flashcards are shown less frequently in order to exploit the psychological spacing effect. The use of spaced repetition has been proven to increase rate of learning.
    Source: Wikipedia .

    You can get a more detailed view in this scientific article by Paul Smolen, Yili Zhang and John H. Bryne (January 25, 2016).

    Task Management

    Task management should be not be neglected when your goal is to boost your productivity. By breaking your work down into smaller, more achievable tasks you will be able to complete larger tasks that initialy may have seemed overwhelming. The smaller tasks should be very specific and attainable within a relatively short period of time. This way you will eliminate procrastination and start wokring on your tasks with extreme focus.

    For more details check out: Baldwin Center For Learning And Teaching and the University Of Georgia.

    How to use the app

    • Add a task.
    • Press Start to begin your session.
    • Work on your task. It's important that you dont come back to check the remaining time. This way you will keep your focus.
    • When the time expires you will get notified by a gentle sound. Make sure your press Start on the short break menu before starting your break.
    • Now it is time to relax, talk to someone, have a snack or exercise a little. After the break timer expires, go back to step 2.
    • After completing 3-4 sessions, take a long break and repeat from step 2.
